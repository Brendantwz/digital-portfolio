My top-3 Movies of all-time

Forrest Gump

"Forrest Gump" is a heartwarming and inspiring film that follows the life of Forrest Gump, a kind-hearted and simple-minded man. Despite his low IQ, Forrest embarks on incredible adventures, including becoming a college football star, serving in the Vietnam War, and starting a successful shrimping business. Along the way, he forms deep friendships, including his lifelong love for Jenny, and teaches us that kindness, perseverance, and being true to oneself can lead to a life filled with meaning and unexpected success.

Lord of the Rings

"The Lord of the Rings" is an epic fantasy film trilogy based on J.R.R. Tolkien's beloved novels. The story follows Frodo Baggins, a hobbit entrusted with a powerful ring that must be destroyed to save Middle-earth from the dark lord Sauron. Frodo embarks on a perilous journey, accompanied by a diverse fellowship of warriors, wizards, and elves, as they face dangerous creatures and epic battles, ultimately culminating in a monumental quest to destroy the ring and restore peace to the land.

Hacksaw Ridge

"Hacksaw Ridge" is a gripping war film based on the true story of Desmond Doss, a pacifist and conscientious objector during World War II. Despite facing ridicule and opposition, Doss serves as a medic on the front lines of the Battle of Okinawa without carrying a weapon. Through his unwavering bravery and selflessness, Doss saves countless lives and becomes a symbol of courage and compassion in the face of adversity.
